# NYC Transit Overtime & Labor Cost Analysis (Power BI)

## Overview
This project analyzes overtime usage and labor costs using publicly available NYC payroll data. The goal is to understand how overtime hours and overtime spending vary across boroughs and job titles, and to measure how dependent operations are on overtime overall.

The analysis is designed to mirror the type of budget and workforce analysis performed in transit operations, such as NYCT Buses.

## Key Findings
- Overtime represents **12.6%** of total labor hours  
- Manhattan and Queens account for the highest overtime hours and overtime costs  
- A small number of job titles drive a large share of overtime spending  
- Boroughs with higher regular hours also tend to have higher overtime usage
